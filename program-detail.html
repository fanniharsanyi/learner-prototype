Registered Nursing (RN)

Published on 05.05.2024

Connecting Southern California colleges to expand access to health sciences programs — from nursing to pharmacy technology.

About the program

This pathway lets you take qualifying courses across member colleges in the Southern CA Health Sciences Network and apply them toward Registered Nursing prerequisites. Enroll directly through the CVC Exchange and your progress is tracked here automatically.
