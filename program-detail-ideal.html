About this program

What you'll learn and why it matters

This collaborative pathway lets you complete prerequisite and core coursework across partner colleges in Southern California, then apply it toward Registered Nursing prerequisites. You'll build a foundation in patient care, clinical reasoning, and the sciences behind modern nursing practice — with advising support at every step.
